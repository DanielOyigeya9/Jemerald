Where Catering Dreams Begin

Founded by Attah John with a vision to revolutionize culinary education in Nigeria, JEMERAD Catering Services is Otupko's newest premier institution for gastronomic excellence.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, internationally-trained faculty, and industry partnerships provide an unparalleled learning environment for aspiring chefs and culinary professionals.

As a new institution, we're committed to nurturing talent through hands-on experience, theoretical knowledge, and exposure to global culinary trends, preparing our students for successful careers anywhere in the world.

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art learning environments are designed to simulate real-world catering workplaces

Commercial Kitchens

Six fully equipped commercial kitchens with industry-standard equipment from global brands for large-scale catering.

Culinary Library

Extensive collection of catering cookbooks, culinary journals, and digital resources for research and event planning.

Demo Theater

State-of-the-art demonstration theater with overhead mirrors and 4K recording capabilities for event presentations.

Herb Garden

On-site herb and vegetable garden providing fresh ingredients and educational opportunities for sustainable catering.

Beverage Lab

Specialized lab for learning mixology, wine pairing, and beverage service for events and catering.

Event Setup Area

Dedicated space for learning event setup, decoration, and logistics for large-scale catering operations.
