Where Catering Dreams Begin

Founded by the Attah's with a vision to revolutionize culinary education in Nigeria, JEMERA Catering Services is Otupko's premier institution for gastronomic excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower aspiring chefs and hospitality professionals with world-class culinary education through our comprehensive catering program. We are dedicated to nurturing creativity, discipline, and excellence in catering, ensuring our graduates are equipped with the skills and confidence to thrive in the global food industry.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading center of culinary excellence, recognized for producing innovative, skilled, and service-oriented catering professionals who redefine standards in hospitality and cuisine both locally and internationally.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, internationally-trained faculty, and industry partnerships provide an unparalleled learning environment for aspiring chefs and culinary professionals.

We're committed to nurturing talent through hands-on experience, theoretical knowledge, and exposure to global culinary trends, preparing our students for successful careers anywhere in the world.
